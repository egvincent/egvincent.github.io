Stanford Open Designs
by Eric Vincent

The Stanford Open is an annual 3 day gymnastics competition that my old gymnastics club hosts for over 800 gymnasts and close to 3000 spectators. Each year from 2011 to 2015, I created an original design for use on the web site, tee shirts, banners, program covers, and other pieces of merchandise. I sketched out ideas, made mock-ups, and vectored and shaded each design using Adobe Illustrator, Inkscape, and other tools.
